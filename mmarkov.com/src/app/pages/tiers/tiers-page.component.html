Altogether, MMARKOV generates TBD estimates per fight. All the estimates can be divided into the following categories:

  • winner
  • method of victory
  • duration
  • judging
  • stats

Within the winner-category the winner is predicted. These estimates are available at the fight, round, minute, and second level.
The manner in which a fighter becomes the winner is addressed in the method of victory-category. This involves knockouts, submissions and judging decisions. In the duration-category, the focus is on when the fight ends. The judging-category provides insights into the judges' scoring if the fight goes to the distance. The stats-category breaks down fight-level performance metrics on an absolute and relative level.

There are 4 different tiers and each tier has it owns granularity.
