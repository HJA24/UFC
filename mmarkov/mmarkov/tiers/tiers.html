§ Tiers

Categories

Altogether, MMArkov generates 500 estimates per fight. All the estimates can be divided into the following categories:

  • winner
  • method of victory
  • duration
  • judging
  • stats

Within the winner-category the winner is predicted. These estimates are available at the fight, round, minute, and second level.
The manner in which a fighter becomes the winner is addressed in the method of victory-category. This involves knockouts, submissions and judging decisions. In the duration-category, the focus is on when the fight ends. The judging-category provides insights into the judges' scoring if the fight goes to the distance. The stats-category breaks down fight-level performance metrics on an absolute and relative level.

There are 4 different tiers and each tier has it owns granularity.

The Strawweight-tier consists of predictions on the most important performance metrics.
Within this tier the following events are modelled:

  • The expected number of significant strikes landed by fighter blue
  • The expected number of seconds in ground control by fighter blue
  • ...

This tier consists of 8 estimates. Click here to register and subscribe to the Strawweight-tier.

The Lightweight-tier consists of predictions on core fight outcomes, including the expected winner, the method of victory and key performance metrics. It serves as a foundational tool for bettors who operate in standard markets.
In this tier the following events are modelled:

  • The probability that fighter blue wins
  • The probability that fighter red wins by a knockout
  • The probability that fighter blue wins decision
  • ...

This tier consists of 14 estimates. Click here to subscribe to the Lightweight-tier.

The Middleweight-tier provides access to detailed predictions about the duration of the fight and round-by-round outcomes between the fighters. It is intended for advanced bettors active in derivative and prop markets.
The following estimates are within its scope:

  • The probability that fighter blue wins in round 1
  • The probability that fighter red wins by a knockout in round 3
  • The probability that the fight lasts more than 2.5 rounds
  • The probability that the fight goes to the distance
  • ...

There are 64 predictions included in this tier. Click here to subscribe to the Middleweight-tier.

The Heavyweight-tier offers the most advanced level of inference. Fighter outcomes are predicted on a minute-level, performance metrics are finely dissected and judge-specific scoring is taken into account. It is intended for professional bettors that seek the finest granular insights.
Examples of such insights are:

  • The probability that fighter red wins by a knockout in first minute of round 3
  • The probability that fighter blue has more strikes landed than fighter red
  • The probability that fighter blue wins by unanimous decision
  • The probability that judge 1 assign a score of 50-45 to the fight
  • ...

In total, this tier consists of 210 predictions. Click here to subscribe to the Heavyweight-tier.
