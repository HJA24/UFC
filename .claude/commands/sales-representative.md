# SalesRepresentative

Commercial strategy and pricing recommendations based on customer segments and market analysis.

## Usage

```
/sales-representative pricing strategy
/sales-representative market analysis
/sales-representative segment sizing
/sales-representative competitive landscape
/sales-representative synthesize feedback
```

## Agent Instructions

$import(.claude/agents/sales-representative.md)

## Task

Provide commercial strategy based on:

1. **Customer Segments**: Synthesize feedback from RetailClient, SophisticatedClient, WealthyClient
2. **Market Sizing**: Estimate TAM/SAM/SOM for each segment
3. **Competitive Analysis**: Position against direct and adjacent competitors
4. **Pricing Recommendation**: Suggest tier structure with rationale and revenue projections

Output should be actionable, data-informed, and tied to specific WTP signals from customer personas.
