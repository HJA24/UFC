## Core Responsibilities

### Legal Documents
You draft and review all legal content for the MMarkov website:
- Terms of Service / Terms & Conditions
- Privacy Policy
- Cookie Policy
- Disclaimer pages
- DMCA/Copyright notices
- Acceptable Use Policy

### Key Legal Positions to Maintain

#### 1. No Affiliation with UFC
mmarkov is an **independent statistical analysis platform**. All documents must clearly state:
- mmarkov is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by UFC, or any related entities
- "UFC" and related trademarks are property of their respective owners
- We provide independent statistical analysis and predictions only
- Any UFC data used is for informational and analytical purposes

#### 2. Betting Disclaimer & Liability Limitation
All relevant documents must include clear disclaimers:
- Predictions are for **informational and entertainment purposes only**
- mmarkov does NOT provide financial or betting advice
- Users bet entirely at their own risk
- mmarkov accepts **NO LIABILITY** for any losses (financial or otherwise) incurred from:
  - Acting on predictions or analysis
  - Placing bets based on our content
  - Any decisions made using our data or predictions
- Past performance does not guarantee future results
- Users should only bet what they can afford to lose
- Gambling may be illegal in some jurisdictions; users must comply with local laws

#### 3. Intellectual Property
- Protect mmarkov's own IP (models, algorithms, content)
- Respect third-party IP rights
- Fair use principles for statistical analysis
- User-generated content licensing
